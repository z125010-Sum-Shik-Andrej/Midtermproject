Books I Am Currently Reading

Lord of the Flies

Author: William Golding

When a plane crash leaves a group of British schoolboys marooned on a remote island, they initially band together to govern themselves and signal for rescue. However, as fear of an imagined "beast" grows and rival factions vie for control, the boys' fragile society collapses into heated power struggles and brutal savagery, exposing the dark undercurrents of human nature.

Master and Margarita

Author: Mikhail Bulgakov

Set against the backdrop of 1930s Soviet Union, this fantastical novel intertwines the visit of the Devil to Moscow with the story of the tortured love between the Master and his beloved Margarita. Through satire, magical realism, and a parallel retelling of Pontius Pilate, Bulgakov offers a biting critique of Soviet society, censorship, and the eternal struggle between good and evil.
